OUR STORY

Expert Mind Center was founded by Mark Wendel Aliser Murillo in 2016 as Learning Assistant, a network of online tutors providing academic support. Over time, the center expanded its services, focusing on reading interventions, academic tutorials, and thesis writing support. In 2025, it officialy registered as ExpertMind Learning and Development Center to further its mission of empowering learniners through holistic, faith-driven education, and specialized services in Special Education, Psychological Assessments, and Counseling in the future.

OUR VISION AND MISSION

The Expert Mind Center envisions a future where learners are empowered through holistic, faith-driven education, guided by our EXPERT Mind Core Values. We are committed to providing inclusive services and specialized support that foster academic excellence, personal growth, and ethical leadership. Through these efforts, we aim to equip students, educators, and organizations to thrive in an ever-changing world and contribute positively to society.
